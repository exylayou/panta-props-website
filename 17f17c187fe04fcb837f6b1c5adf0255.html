Here is the updated Product Requirements Document for "Panta Props - Playful Pop," incorporating the WhatsApp-first commerce model and service segmentation. ## Current PRD # Panta Props - Playful Pop ## Product Overview **The Pitch:** High-end photo booth props that ditch tacky cardboard for durable, camera-ready PVC and acrylic. Panta Props turns event photography into a tactile, premium experience with gear that survives the party. **For:** Event planners, professional photo booth operators, and couples who refuse to compromise on their wedding aesthetic. **Device:** Desktop & Mobile (Optimized for WhatsApp-first conversion) **Design Direction:** "Playful Pop" — A tactile, high-energy aesthetic combining rounded geometries with vibrant gradients. It feels like a candy store for event gear: bright, squishy, and impossible not to touch. **Inspired by:** Headspace (rounded, friendly UI), Snapbar (playful corporate), Superplastic (premium vinyl toy aesthetic). --- ## Screens - **Home:** High-energy introduction with clear segmentation between "Stocked" and "Custom" paths. - **Stocked Shop / Collection:** Grid-based browsing experience for ready-to-ship items with chat-based purchasing. - **Product Detail:** Deep dive into material quality (thickness, matte finish) and bundle details. - **Custom Services Landing:** Informational page explaining the bespoke design process (Consultation -> Design -> Production). - **Brand Story:** Narrative page contrasting "floppy cardboard" with "Panta durability." - **Inquiry Success:** Reassurance modal/page after clicking WhatsApp links, explaining next steps. --- ## Key Flows **Flow: The "Wedding Hashtag" Inquiry** 1. User lands on **Home** -> selects "Custom Bespoke Design" path in hero. 2. User lands on **Custom Services Landing** -> reads about the design process. 3. User clicks "Get Quote via WhatsApp" -> WhatsApp opens with pre-filled message: *"Hi Panta! I'm interested in a custom wedding prop..."* **Flow: The Professional Restock** 1. User lands on **Shop** -> selects "Material: Acrylic" filter. 2. User hovers over "Neon Party Set" -> Image flips to show side-profile thickness. 3. User clicks card -> navigates to **Product Detail**. 4. User clicks "Buy via WhatsApp". 5. WhatsApp opens with specific item reference -> User sends message to finalize payment/shipping. ---
Design System ## Color Palette - **Primary:** `#E02E76` - Vibrant Magenta (Primary Buttons, Price Tags, Hero Accents) - **Secondary:** `#0F82C4` - Electric Blue (Secondary Buttons, Links, Hover States) - **WhatsApp Green:** `#25D366` - Specific CTA background for conversion buttons. - **Background:** `#F5F5F7` - Cool Off-White (Main Page Background) - **Surface:** `#FFFFFF` - Product Cards, Navbar, Modals - **Text:** `#2E3134` - Charcoal Grey (Headings, Body) - **Muted:** `#8D9399` - Subtitles, Footer Text, Borders - **Accent:** `#FFD166` - "New" Badges, Star Ratings, Highlights ## Typography Fonts chosen for rounded terminals and high x-heights to match the "friendly" vibe. - **Headings:** **Fredoka**, 700/600. Round, bouncy, geometric. - H1: 64px (Tight tracking `-0.02em`) - H2: 48px - H3: 32px - **Body:** **Nunito**, 500/700. Rounded sans-serif, highly legible but characterful. - Body Large: 18px - Body Regular: 16px - **UI:** **Quicksand**, 600. For buttons, badges, and small labels. - Button Text: 16px (All caps, wide tracking `0.05em`) **Style notes:** - **Buttons:** 40px radius (Full pill shape). - **Cards:** 24px border radius. Deep, soft shadows to simulate "floating": `box-shadow: 0 12px 32px rgba(15, 130, 196, 0.08)`. - **Texture:** Subtle confetti pattern SVG overlay on the `#F5F5F7` background at 3% opacity. ## Design Tokens ```css :root { --color-primary: #E02E76; --color-secondary: #0F82C4; --color-whatsapp: #25D366; --color-bg: #F5F5F7; --color-surface: #FFFFFF; --color-text: #2E3134; --font-display: 'Fredoka', sans-serif; --font-body: 'Nunito', sans-serif; --radius-pill: 9999px; --radius-card: 24px; --shadow-float: 0 12px 32px rgba(15, 130, 196, 0.08); --gradient-primary: linear-gradient(135deg, #E02E76 0%, #B01E58 100%); --gradient-secondary: linear-gradient(135deg, #0F82C4 0%, #095C8D 100%); } ```

Screen Specifications ### 1. Home Page **Purpose:** clear routing between Ready-to-Ship inventory and Custom Services. **Layout:** 1. **Sticky Navbar:** White pill-shaped container floating 24px from top. Logo left, Nav center, WhatsApp Contact right. 2. **Split Hero:** Two distinct card-like columns taking up the main view. * *Left (Stock):* "Shop The Drop." Image of stacked, colorful props. CTA: "Browse Stock". * *Right (Custom):* "Make it Yours." Image of a designer sketching a prop. CTA: "Start Custom Order". 3. **Marquee:** Infinite scroll banner: "NO MORE FLOPPY CARDBOARD • 5MM THICK PVC • ANTI-GLARE •". 4. **Category Grid:** "Shop by Vibe" (Wedding, Corporate, Holiday). 5. **Featured Products:** Horizontal scroll of trending items. **Key Elements:** - **Hero Split Cards:** 32px border radius. On hover, the hovered side expands slightly (flex-grow) while the other dims. - **Prop Floaters:** Parallax effect on scroll. 3 distinct PNG layers of props blurring at different depths. - **Category Cards:** Square, 300px. Full bleed image with heavy rounded corners (32px). Text overlay in bottom left: White text, thick black outline. **States:** - **Loading:** Skeleton loaders for hero image and product carousel. - **Hover (Cards):** Scale up 1.05x, shadow intensifies and shifts color to Magenta tint. **Components:** - **NavPill:** Height 64px, Width 90%, Surface White, Shadow Float. --- ### 2. Stocked Shop / Collection Page **Purpose:** Browsing ready-to-ship inventory with direct chat purchasing. **Layout:** 1. **Header:** "All Props" title centered. Gradient text fill (Magenta to Blue). 2. **Sidebar (Left):** Filters (Event, Material, Price). Accordion style with rounded checkboxes. 3. **Grid (Right):** 3-column product grid. **Key Elements:** - **Product Card:** - **Image:** 4:5 aspect ratio. Light grey background `#F0F0F2`. - **Badge:** "Best Seller" pill in top left (`#FFD166` bg). - **Details:** Title (Nunito, 700, 18px). Price (Fredoka, 600, 20px, Magenta). - **Buy Button:** Circle button with `WhatsApp` icon (Green #25D366) floating on image bottom-right. - **Filters:** "Squircle" checkboxes. When checked, fill is `#0F82C4` with white check. **Interactions:** - **Hover Product Image:** Image swaps to "Side Profile" view to demonstrate 5mm thickness. - **Click Buy Button:** Opens WhatsApp web/app with pre-filled message: "Hi! I want to buy [Product Name]..." --- ### 3. Product Detail Page (PDP) **Purpose:** Convincing user of premium material quality and facilitating chat conversion. **Layout:** 1. **Gallery (Left):** Main image + 3 thumbnails below. 2. **Info (Right):** Sticky column. Title, Rating, Price, "Buy via WhatsApp". 3. **Material Deep Dive (Below):** Full-width section focusing on "The Panta Difference" (Anti-glare, Waterproof). **Key Elements:** - **Thickness Visualizer:** A specific graphic showing a coin next to the prop edge to denote 5mm thickness. - **Bundle Selector:** "Make it a Set?" toggle switch. Toggles between "Single Prop ($12)" and "Full Kit ($45)". - **Material Badge:** Icon + Text row: "Waterproof", "Matte Finish", "Shatterproof". - **Primary CTA:** Large Green Button (`#25D366`) with WhatsApp Icon. Text: "Buy on WhatsApp". **Interactions:** - **Click CTA:** Opens WhatsApp with specific message including SKU/Bundle info. --- ### 4. Custom Services Landing (Formerly Builder) **Purpose:** Explain the bespoke service model and generate leads via chat. **Layout:** 1. **Hero:** "Your Brand, Our Props." Large visual of a custom logo prop being held. 2. **Process Steps (Horizontal Row):** * Step 1: "Idea" (Icon: Lightbulb) - "Send us your text, logo, or napkin sketch." * Step 2: "Design" (Icon: Pencil) - "We send a digital proof in 24 hours." * Step 3: "Production" (Icon: Factory) - "Laser cut and shipped in 3 days." 3. **Gallery:** Grid of past custom work (Wedding hashtags, Corporate logos). 4. **Inquiry Section:** Large CTA card. **Key Elements:** - **Process Icons:** Large, bubbly 3D-style icons. - **Inquiry CTA:** "Chat with a Designer". Button opens WhatsApp with message: "Hi, I have a custom design idea..." - **Price Guide:** Simple table showing base pricing for custom sizes (Small: $25, Medium: $45, Large: $75). **States:** - **Hover (Gallery):** Images zoom slightly; overlay appears with the client's name. --- ### 5. Brand Story **Purpose:** Emotional connection + justification of higher price point. **Layout:** 1. **Hero:** Split screen. Left: "Sad Cardboard" (Greyscale, bent). Right: "Happy Panta" (Vibrant, sturdy). Slider handle in middle to compare. 2. **Timeline:** Vertical line with nodes showing company history. 3. **Team Grid:** Circular photos of the team holding props over their faces. **Key Elements:** - **Comparison Slider:** Interactive dragging element. Reveals the "Panta" image over the "Generic" image. - **Stats Row:** 3 large numbers: "50k+ Props Sold", "0 Bent Corners", "100% Recyclable". --- ### 6. Cart / Checkout (Removed) *Note: Traditional cart and checkout screens have been removed in favor of the direct WhatsApp service model.*

Ensure WhatsApp links grab the specific product title dynamically. 6. **Custom Services Page:** Layout the 3-step process clearly and integrate the "Get Quote" WhatsApp link.
